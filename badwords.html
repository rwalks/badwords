BADWORDS
INSTRUCTIONS
1. Clear your cookies.
2. Click on a disguise.
3. Allow the fake website visits to complete.
4. Watch your advertisements change.
ABOUT
Badwords automatically visits a bunch of websites under the guise of a character (Mark Zuckerberg, a Dog, or a Tea Party Weirdo), hiding your actual browsing habits in a sea of "fake" cookies. The targeted ads you see will then match the ad profile of the disguise, instead of you.
